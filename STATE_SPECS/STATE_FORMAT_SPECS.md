# State Transcript Format Specifications — Comparison Reference

**Purpose:** Engine formatting configuration reference for Louisiana, New York, and New Jersey court reporter transcript standards.
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-23
**Sources:** Official state regulations and court administrative documents.

---

## Q&A Formatting Rules

| Rule | Louisiana (Freelance/Deposition) | New York | New Jersey |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Q designation position** | Begins no more than 5 spaces from left margin | 5 spaces indent; Q begins there | 6 spaces from left marginal line (optional period) |
| **Q text start** | Within 5 spaces after the Q designation | 10 spaces from left margin | 11 spaces from left marginal line |
| **A designation position** | Same as Q: no more than 5 spaces from left margin | Same line, 5 spaces indent; A begins there | At the left marginal line (optional period) |
| **A text start** | Within 5 spaces after the A designation | 10 spaces from left margin | 6 spaces from left marginal line |
| **Continuation lines** | Left margin | Left margin | Left marginal line |
| **Block version available?** | Yes — Q&A at left margin, text ≤ 5 spaces after, carryover ≤ 6 spaces | No | No |
| **Each Q&A on separate line?** | Yes | Yes | Yes |

## Colloquy Formatting Rules

| Rule | Louisiana (Freelance/Deposition) | New York | New Jersey |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Speaker ID position** | Within 15 spaces of left margin | 15 spaces from left margin | 11 spaces from left marginal line |
| **After speaker ID** | Colon (then text) | Colon + 2 spaces, then remarks | Colon (text begins 3rd space after colon) |
| **Continuation lines** | 10 spaces from left margin | 5 spaces from left margin | Left marginal line |
| **New paragraphs** | 15 spaces | 15 spaces (return to speaker indent) | 6 spaces from left marginal line |
| **Alternative format** | Speaker ID on separate line (max 10 spaces), text on next line at 15 spaces, continuations at 12 spaces | N/A | N/A |

## Title / Cover Page Requirements

### Louisiana — Deposition (Freelance)
- Court name, parish, state
- Case number and division
- Party names (plaintiff vs. defendant)
- "Deposition of [NAME]" with location, date
- Reporter name, CCR credential, RPR credential

### Louisiana — Official Court Transcript
- Court name, parish, state
- Case number and section
- Cause title (State vs. Defendant)
- Type of proceeding (e.g., Probable Cause Hearing, Motion to Suppress)
- Presiding judge name
- Appearances: parties and attorneys
- Reporter name, CCR/RPR credential and official title

### New York (22 NYCRR § 108.3)
- Court name
- Venue (county/city)
- Case name and accusatory instrument number(s)
- Charges
- Courthouse address
- Proceeding dates
- Presiding authority
- Jury status (jury or non-jury)
- Full appearances of counsel
- Court reporter name(s)
- Interpreter name(s) if applicable

### New Jersey (AOC 2021)
- Type of court (Municipal Court or Superior Court)
- County name and state
- Case docket number (lower court and appellate if applicable)
- The "box" — case title in ALL CAPS, closed with colons or parentheses
- "Box identification" — proceeding type (TRIAL, PLEA, SENTENCE, MOTION) at right of box
- Place — full street address of court/institution
- Date — hearing date(s), with page ranges per proceeding type
- Volume identification (if multi-volume)
- BEFORE: — presiding judge name in ALL CAPS, jury noted if present
- TRANSCRIPT ORDERED BY — ordering party name and firm
- APPEARANCES — attorney names in ALL CAPS with firm in parentheses and party represented
- Court reporter / transcriber name and address (bottom of page, aligned with venue)

| Feature | Louisiana (Deposition) | Louisiana (Court) | New York | New Jersey |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Witness list** | Yes, with examination type and page | Yes, with examination type and page | Yes, by calling party, page(s), examination type | Yes |

## Certificate / Certification Requirements

### Louisiana — Deposition (Freelance)
**Two-part structure:** Reporter's Page + Reporter's Certificate (separate pages)

**Reporter's Page** states:
- Dashes (--) used to indicate pauses, changes in thought, talkovers
- Words/names unverifiable through reference material denoted as "(spelled phonetically)"
- Signed by CCR with credential line

**Reporter's Certificate** certifies:
- Witness was duly sworn by authority of R.S. 37:2554
- Testimony reported in stenotype method
- Prepared/transcribed by reporter or under personal direction
- True and correct transcript to best of ability
- Prepared in compliance with LACSRB transcript format guidelines
- Reporter has no prohibited financial/contractual relationship with any party (per La. C.C.P. Art. 1434)
- Not of counsel, not related to counsel/parties, not interested in outcome
- Sealed with original signature and required seal (certification valid only with original seal)

### Louisiana — Official Court
**Reporter's Page** (same dash/phonetic notation language as deposition)

**Reporter's Certificate** certifies:
- Testimony reported in stenotype method
- Prepared/transcribed by reporter or under personal direction
- True and correct transcript to best of ability
- Prepared in compliance with transcript format guidelines required by statute, board rules, or Supreme Court of Louisiana
- Not of counsel, not related to counsel/parties, not interested in outcome

### New York (22 NYCRR § 108.3)
- Certification language not prescribed verbatim in § 108.3
- Reporter certifies accuracy and compliance with Part 108 specifications
- Must be signed by court reporter
- Payment per page contingent on compliance with specifications and minimum 13 lines of material per page

### New Jersey (AOC 2021)
**Certification placed on last page of transcript (no extra charge if requires separate page)**

**Certified Court Reporter (non-compressed):**
> "I, [Name], C.S.R. License Number [#], an Official Court Reporter in and for the State of New Jersey, do hereby certify the foregoing to be prepared in full compliance with the current Transcript Format for Judicial Proceedings and is a true and accurate non-compressed transcript to the best of my knowledge and ability."
> Signed with: Official Court Reporter, Courthouse, Date, County

**Transcriber (non-compressed):**
> "I, [Name], the assigned transcriber, do hereby certify the foregoing transcript of proceedings on tape number [#], index number from [#] to [#], is prepared in full compliance with the current Transcript Format for Judicial Proceedings and is a true and accurate non-compressed transcript of the proceedings as recorded."
> Signed with: Signature, AOC Number, Agency Name (if applicable), Date

**Compressed format** uses identical language with "compressed transcript" substituted.

## Additional State-Specific Rules

### Louisiana

**Deposition (Freelance) — La. Admin. Code tit. 46, § XXI-1101:**
- No numbered blank lines permitted on regular pages (exceptions: final page, title page, contents, appearances, stipulations, certificates)
- Parentheticals and exhibit markings: begin within 15 spaces of left margin; continuations at 15 spaces
- Quoted material: single or double-spaced; follows Q&A or colloquy formatting
- Effective June 1995; amended December 1996

**Official Court — La. R.S. 13:961.2:**
- 32 lines per page on legal paper (8.5" x 14")
- Min. 9 cpi (Pica type)
- Left margin ≤ 1"; right margin ≤ 1"
- Certificate must state method of reporting (e.g., "utilizing the digital recording method")
- Certificate number required under signature

**Court transcript format (sample):**
- Line numbers 1–32 on left side
- Q begins at left margin; text begins after "Q." on same line
- Colloquy: Speaker name in ALL CAPS with colon, text follows on same line
- Witness examination header: "- DIRECT EXAMINATION -" / "- CROSS-EXAMINATION -" centered
- Attribution header: "BY MR./MS. [NAME]:" flush left

### New York

**22 NYCRR § 108.3 additional rules:**
- Index: single index (or separate index per reporter) listing witnesses by calling party
- Examination types must be listed (direct, cross, redirect, recross, etc.)
- Exhibits listed with ID and introduction page references
- Title page includes full case caption with all required elements
- Transcribed material must extend as closely as possible to the right margin
- Payment per page not allowed unless page has at least 13 lines of material AND complies with all specifications

### New Jersey

**AOC 2021 additional rules:**
- Non-compressed: double-spaced; compressed: single-spaced (2 pages per sheet)
- Compressed transcripts: each sheet = 2 transcript pages; up to 200 sheets per volume
- Page heading on EVERY page (not counted as a transcript line): last name of witness + examination type (e.g., "L. Hannan - Cross/Redirect")
- If two parties share a last name, include first initial in heading
- Paragraphs begin at 6th space from left marginal line
- Depositions read at trial: use Q&A format with quotation marks bounding each Q&A paragraph
- Off-the-record: noted in parentheses, centered on page
- Descriptive/non-verbal material: placed in parentheses, kept to one line if possible, centered
- Verdicts: verbatim polling required in notes; unanimous verdicts do not list each juror response in transcript
- Spelling reference: Webster's International Dictionary, Black's Law Dictionary, Tabor's Medical Dictionary
- Electronic filing: NO preprinted top/bottom horizontal marginal lines (vertical lines only)
- Dot matrix printers: 24-pin head required; 9-pin not acceptable
- Paper weight: minimum 13 pounds (originals and copies)
